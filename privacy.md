GlowQR – Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

GlowQR (“the App”) is developed by Jonathan Wright (“we”, “us”, “our”).
This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

GlowQR does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

We do not collect:

Names

Email addresses

Phone numbers

Photos or media

Contact lists

Location data

Usage analytics

Device identifiers

QR code contents

Any data stored in the app

All QR codes and settings remain localized to your device only.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

GlowQR stores the following solely on your device:

QR codes you create

QR codes you scan

Selected themes

Pro unlock status

This information:

Is stored locally

Is not transmitted to any server

Is deleted when you uninstall the app or delete items manually

We never have access to this data.

3. Camera Usage

GlowQR requires camera access only when using the built-in QR scanner.

The camera feed stays on device

It is not recorded or transmitted

It is used only to detect QR codes in real-time

4. In-App Purchases

GlowQR offers a one-time upgrade to “GlowQR Pro”.

All purchases are processed by Apple’s App Store.
We do not receive or store:

Credit card numbers

Billing information

Apple ID details

Apple handles all purchase authentication and security.

5. Third-Party Services

GlowQR uses Google AdMob to show advertisements for free users.

AdMob may collect:

Device identifiers

Approximate (coarse) location

Interaction with ads

Diagnostics and performance data

AdMob’s data practices are governed by Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Important:
Once GlowQR Pro is unlocked, the app disables all advertising, and AdMob is no longer active.

No other third-party SDKs or analytics tools are used.

6. Children’s Privacy

GlowQR does not collect personal data and does not target children.
The app contains no accounts, messaging, or social features.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as the app evolves.
Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
